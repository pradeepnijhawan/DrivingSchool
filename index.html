About Me

At FOREVER DRIVING SCHOOL,Our driving trainer brings over 8 years of extensive experience in teaching and guiding new drivers. With a strong track record of helping learners become confident, safe, and responsible drivers, their expertise ensures that each student receives top-quality instruction tailored to their needs. We strive to create a welcoming and supportive learning environment for our students. Our mission is to provide top-notch instruction and guidance throughout the driving lesson, ensuring that students feel confident and prepared before the road test. Driving can be compared to walking, as walking is second nature to us and requires minimal conscious effort. Similarly for driving, new drivers must undergo a mental transition to ensure smooth eye and hand coordination. Without this, mistakes can occur on the road due to overcompensation of vehicle controls.
